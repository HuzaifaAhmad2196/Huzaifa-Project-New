Connections between EYLF, NQS and Australian Curriculum

At every stage of the early literacy development, it is evident to find connections with national structures and standards:

  • The EYLF has identified communication as a core part in the learning and development of children (Outcome 5) and the significance of cultural identity and belonging (Outcome 1).
  • The NQS emphasizes involvement of programs containing communication into play (QA1), responsive and respectful relationships (QA5), and partnership with families (QA6).
  • The Australian Curriculum (English F2), phonics, oral language, reading, and writing are considered essential to future literacy achievement.
